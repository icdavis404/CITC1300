Mission Types

Primary Objecyives

Primary objectives are the main goal of Helldiver missions. They can range from evacuating civilians to launching a missile. Some primary objectives have parts that must be completed before the main objective can be completed. Upon completion of the primary objective, the Helldivers are able to call in extraction and return to the super destroyer. If the mission clock runs out, the primary objective is failed, stratagems are disabled, and an emergency extration shuttle is dispatched. Most missions are on a 40 minute timer, but a select few have a lower time.

Universal Objectives

  • Spread Democracy

    For this objective, Helldivers are tasked with raising flags and destroying enemy structures.

  • Conduct Geological Survey

    For this objective, Helldivers must first verify the ore vein location and then acquire the soil scan data.

  • Launch ICBM

    For this objective, Helldivers are tasked with launching an intercontinental ballistic missile. Before launching it, they must find the launch codes and reactivate the power generator. After this, they are able to raise the missile from its silo and launch it. The missile will detonate in the distance, sending a shockwave over the mission area.

  • Retrieve Valuable Data

    For this objective, Helldivers must collect encrypted hard drives from around the map and carry them to a transmission site. Before transmitting, they must reactivate the power generator.

  • Blitz: Search and Destroy

    For this objective, Helldivers are tasked with destroying a certain number of enemy bases (Automaton fabricators, Terminid hives, or Illuminate warp ships). These missions are 12 minutes rather than 40.

  • Emergency Evacuation

    For this objective, Helldivers must evacuate civilians from bunkers to an evacuation shuttle. Before evacuating them though, they must establish communication uplinks. After this, they are then able to escort trapped civilians from bunkers to the shuttle bay on the planet.

  • Retrieve Essential Personnel

    This objective is only available during planetary defense campaigns. For this objective, Helldivers must evacuate personnel from bunkers to a shuttle bay. It is very similar to the emergency evacuation, but without the communication uplinks. This mission is on a 15 minute timer.

  • Evacuate High-Value Assets

    For this objective, Helldivers must protect rockets while they launch. Enemies will attempt to attack and overrun the complex, preventing the recovery of high value assets. Rockets will launch during this mission, and more enemies will appear after each launch.

  • Eradicate Missions

    This missions are currently only available on the automaton and terminid fronts. The Helldivers must eradicate swarms of enemies. After the set goal is achieved, the Helldivers are able to extract.

Terminid Objectives

  • Eliminate Missions

    For this objective, Helldivers are tasked with eliminating a specific target. These include brood commanders, bile titans, chargers, and impalers. Brood commanders are heavy units that use swarm and rush attacks, and also call upon reinforcements when attacked. Bile titans are massive enemies that spew acid at Helldivers. Chargers charge towards and attempt to crush Helldivers. Impalers are possibly the most dangerous of all; they are massive, heavily armored units that call tentacles from the ground to attack Helldivers.

  • Activate E-710 Pumps

    E-710 is the fuel that keeps the Super Earth war machine running. Without it, Super Earth would collapse. For this objective, Helldivers must reactivate a number of E-710 pump stations.

  • Enable E-710 Extraction

    Similarly to the afformentioned activate E-710 pumps mission, Helldivers must reactivate a number of E-710 pumps. After this though, they must load the E-710 into a shuttle for it to be transported off-world.

  • Purge Hatcheries

    For this objective, Helldivers must purge terminid hatcheries to prevent the terminids from reproducing. Rampant, unchecked terminid reproduction leads to the spread of terminid spores throughout the galaxy.

  • Nuke Nursery

    For this objective, Helldivers must destroy terminid nurseries located deep below ground. They must call in a drill, drill through to the nursery and deploy a nuke to destroy it. The goal of this mission is similar to that of the purge hatcheries mission.

  • Activate/Deactivate Terminid Control System (TCS)

    These missions were available during an operation on the planet Meridia. The goal was to cull the Terminid population on Meridia and allow for resettlement. This objective failed when the gas used by the TCS caused mutations in the local Terminid population. This reulted in the need for the system to be deactivated. During these missions, Helldivers had to wait for towers to charge before engaging.

  • Deploy Dark Fluid

    After the Terminid Control system failed, it as necessary to destroy Meridia to prevent the spread of the terminids to neighboring planets. Mysterious dark fluid was injeceted into the planet until it reached critical mass and collapsed into a black hole.

Automaton Objectives

  • Destroy Transmission Network

    Helldivers are tasked with destroying antennae located around the mission area in order to inhibit automaton communications.

  • Eliminate Missions

    For this objective, Helldivers are tasked with eliminating a specific target. These include devastators, hulks, and factory striders. Devastators are a medium tier enemy with medium armor. They can be difficult to eliminate due to their numbers and the fact some are equipped with rocket launchers. Hulks are large robots that are equipped with weapons from buzzsaws to rockets. Their heavy armor makes them difficult to defeat, but the weakspots on their backs and head can make it much easier. Factory striders are massive walking factories. They are heavily armed, and produce devastators. Typically, heacy ordnance is required for their destruction.

  • Sabotage Supply Bases

    For this mission, Helldivers are tasked with destroying automaton fuel and ammunition depots. They are able to use hellbombs(large bombs that must be manually armed in order to destroy a target) in order to achieve their destruction.

  • Sabotage Air Base

    For this mission, Helldivers must destroy automaton dropships on the grounds and control towers. Most explosive stratagems work to destroy the dropships, but hellbombds are available. Destroying the control tower is trickier, the Helldivers must insert a hard drive into the terminal and activate a number of systems, causing the tower to explode.

  • Destroy Command Bunkers

    For these missions, Helldivers must destroy the automaton command bunkers. These bunkers are heavily armed and armored, so it is dangerous to approach. Turrets on the bunker will target helldivers, inhibiting their approach. Few weapons are able to destroy the bunker, so hellbombes are available.

  • Neutralize Orbital Defenses

    For these missions, Helldivers must destroy the automaton's orbital cannons. Hellbombs are used to fulfill this goal.

Illuminate Objectives

  • Retrieve Recon Craft Intel

    For this mission, Helldivers must recover the black box from a crashed reconnaisance plane. They must then transport it to a transmission station and transmit its data to the super destroyer in orbit.

  • Free Colony

    These missions are similar to the spread democracy missions. Helldivers must raise the flag of Super Earth and destroy Illuminate monoliths.

  • Destroy Harvesters

    Similar to the eliminate missions for the terminids and automatons, Helldivers must destroy Illuminate harvesters for this missions. Harvesters are massive, three-legged machines equipped with laser weapons and shields. Helldivers must first disable the shield, and then they are able to destroy the harvester itself.

Secondary Objectives

Secondary objectives are optional side missions that can be completed to gain more experience or provide a useful tool for the mission. These missions are not necessary to succesfully complete the main objective, but they are extemely useful in doing so. They are also excpetionally helpful in finding more samples or eliminating enemies. (In this section SEAF refers to the Super Earth Armed Forces)

Universal Objectives

  • SEAF Artillery

    This side objective enables artillery that the Helldivers can use to further their mission. The Helldivers must first activate the console, then load shells, and finally enable the connection the the super destroyer. After which, they are able to call in strikes from the artillery using a stratagem. Explosive, smoke, and napalm are just some of the available ammo types.

  • LiDAR Station

    This side objective shows all points of interest on the map. To complete it, Helldivers must raise the radar dish and align it. This is one of the most useful side objectives available.

  • SEAF SAM Site

    This side objective is available on the Automaton and Illuminate fronts, and once armed, shoots down enemy ships within its targeting radius. This means that enemies are not able to reach the ground, making it safer for the Helldivers. To complete this objective, the Helldivers must raise the turret and enable its subsystems.

  • Upload Escape Pod Data

    For this side objective, Helldivers must find a crashed escape pod and upload its data.

  • Destroy Rogue Research Station

    For this side objective, Helldivers must destroy a research station that has gone rogue using a hellbomb or other heavy ordnance.

  • Terminate Illegal Broadcast

    For this side objective, Helldivers must destroy a broadcasst tower that is transmitting enemy propaganda. To do so, they can use most explosive grenades and stratagems.

Terminid Objectives

  • Spore Spewer

    Spore spewer are large terminid structures similar in appearance to a mushroom. They emit spores into the air, obscuring vision. To destroy them, Helldivers must use a support weapon such as an autocannon or recoilless rifle to destroy it from a distance. ALternativaly, they can use stratagems up close to destroy it.

  • Stalker Nest

    Stalkers are a type of terminid able to go invisible and pounce on Helldivers. They are difficult to deal with, and eliminating their nest should be priority #1. To do so, a Helldiver must throw a grenade into the nest or destroy it with a stratagem. Once the nest is destroyed, stalkers will stop spawning from it.

  • Shrieker Nest

    Shriekers are flyinh terminids that swoop down from above to attack helldivers. They are less dangerous than stalkers, but can be overwhelming in large numbers. Their nests are located in groups of three, and can be destroyed similarly to a spore spewer. Recoilles rifles, autocannons and explosive stratagems are very effective.

  • Retrieve Mutant Larva

    For this side objective, Helldivers must recover and extract with a canister containing a mutated terminid larva. Once the canister is picked up, terminids will swarm the Helldiver wearing it in a vain attempt to recover it.

Automaton Objectives

  • Detector Tower

    Detector towers are automaton structures that will scan for Helldivers and upon seeing them, call in reinforcements. To destroy them, Helldivers must use heavy ordnance such as hellbombs or orbital precision strikes.

  • Mortar Emplacement

    Mortar emplacements are dangerous objectives that will rain explosive shells down on Helldivers once they are in the targeting radius. To destroy them, Helldivers must use grenades or stratagems in order to destroy the turrets.

  • Stratagem Jammer

    A Stratagem jammer disables all Helldiver stratagems once they are in its radius. They are frustrating structure and can prove to be quite a challenge. Without stratagems, Helldivers must rely on their primary and support weapons to fight their way to the jammer console. Once at the console, they must disable the jammer, after which they can destroy it with a hellbomb.

  • Anti-Air Emplacement

    Anti-air emplacements prevent eagles strike fighters from flying through their targeting radius, and thus disable eagle stratagems within that area. Destroying them is similar to mortar emplacements. Helldivers must use grenades or stratagems in order to destroy the turrets.

  • Gunship Facility

    Gunship facilites produce automaton gunships. These gunships fly high above the battlefield, raining fire upon the Helldivers on the ground. The gunships are difficult to destroy, and Helldivers must use support weapons such as the recoilless rifle or autocannon to do so. The facility can be destroyed using a hellbomb.

Illuminate Objectives

  • Purge Illuminate

    For this side mission, Helldivers must eliminate a certain number of Illuminate overseers. Overseers are well armored and can cause damage to Helldivers easily. Overseers can be eliminated with extended fire, explosives, or stratagems; elevated overseers can also be eliminated by targeting their jetpack..

  • Cognitive Disruptor

    Cognitive disruptors scramble stratagem codes making it more difficult to call in support. They can be disabled by destroying their power connections. These can be destroyed in nearly any way whether it be a grenade, stratagem, support weapon, or primary weapons.
