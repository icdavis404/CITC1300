The Terminids

The Terminids are a species of bug-like creatures that attempt to spread by any means necessary. Throughout the Second Galactic War, new variants have evolved making them a more formidable adversary. The lowest level of Terminids sucha as the scavengers and pouncers swarm helldivers and attempt to overrun them. Scavengers are small bugs that will attack helldivers with their claws in large numbers. Pouncers are similar, except they have increased agility when compared to scavenegrs. Medium bugs such as warriors and hive guards are bulkier, meaning they are more resilient to the attacks of helldivers. Warriors are larger bugs that have better armor and will attack similarly to scavengers. Hive guards have an armored front, meaning bullets from most guns will ricochet off. Helldivers must aim in between the armor or flank them. Heavy Terminids such as chargers or brood commanders. are heavily armored, and helldivers will likely need to use support weapons to eliminate them. Chargers are large bugs with heavy armor impervious to nearly all weapons. Only explosives or shots to their soft underbelly can defeat them. Brood commanders are larger, more heavily armored versions of warriors. They will charge and stun helldivers, and are resilient against all attacks, so they can be difficult to defeat. Elite Terminids such as bile titans and impalers are massive enemies. Bile titans are tall bugs that spew acid at Helldivers. They are heavily armored and out of reach of some weapons due to their height. Impalers are massive creatures that call tentacels from the ground that attack Helldivers. Elite enemies require heavy ordnance in order to defeat them. Screechers are flying enemies that will swoop down upon Helldivers in large swarms. They are fragile, but their speed and numbers can make them difficult to defeat. The Terminids are farmed in order to provide the E-710 Super Earth needs as fuel. They are a formidable enemy that will stop at nothing to destroy Super Earth.

The Automaton Legion

The Automaton Legion are a group of rogue robots set on destroying Super Earth. They are descended from teh cyborgs that endangered Super Earth during the First Galactic War. Low-level enemies such as troopers and Commissars will fire upon Helldivers with laser weapons, sometimes using mounted laser cannons. Comissars are especially dangerous because they can call in reinforcements on their location using a flare. Scout striders are troopers riding on a walker; they are dangerous from the front due to their frontal armor and laser cannons, but are vulnerable from the sides and rear. Berserkers are mid-level Automatons with chainsaws for arms. They are resilient and very dangerous at close range. Devastators are mid-level Autaomatons with medium armor, meaning many weapons can not damage them. They primarily use laser weapons, however some versions are equipped with rockets or shields. Hulks are large, heavily armed robots equipped with a variety of weapons. They are impervious to most fire, however they have a number of weakpoints that can be exploited by Helldivers. The Automatons have a number of tanks that are heavily armed and armored. They can be equipped with heavy cannons, multi-barreled machine guns, and multiple-launch rocket systems. Automaton gunships fly high above helldivers and fire at them with machine guns and rockets. Their armor and distance make them formidable enemies. The Automatons can also have a number of weapons emplacements such as mortars or ani-aircraft guns.

The Illuminate

The Illuminate are an ancient, highky advanced alien race set on invading and destroying Super Earth. They were repelled during the First Galactic War, but they returned to challenge the might of Super Earth and the Helldivers once again. The voteless are a low-level enemy made up of former citizens of Super Earth that have been taken over by the Illuminate's mind control. They will attack helldivers in large swarms, running and leaping at them. Unfortunately, they are too far gone under the Illuminate's mind control and there is no way to save them. Overseers and elevated overseers are squid-like creatures that will use laser weapons and explosives against the Helldivers. Elevated overseers are equipped with jetpacks that allow them to stay far away from Helldivers, but present a weak point to be exploited. Watchers are flying drones that can attack helldivers, but their primary danger is that they are able to call in Illuminate reinforcements. Harvesters are large tri-pedal robots equipped with shield generators and laser beam weapons.
